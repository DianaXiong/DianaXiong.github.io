Biography

I am currently a researcher fellow in HP-NTU Digital Manufacturing Corporate Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Jianmin Zheng.

I obtained my Ph.D. in the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Pedro V. Sander and Prof. Ajay Joneja. And I received my B. Engineering. in Software Engineering from Hua Zhong University of Science and Technology.
