Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor within VCC, Shenzhen University. Before that, I was an Associate Researcher in Guangdong Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence and Digital Economy (SZ), a Research Fellow in HP-NTU Digital Manufacturing Corporate Lab under the supervision of Prof. Jianmin Zheng, and a R&D engineer in Tencent (SSP offer).

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Pedro V. Sander and Prof. Ajay Joneja. And I received my B. Engineering. in Software Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research interests include computer graphics, architectural geometry, and artificial intelligence. In particular, our group focus on topics related to texture generation/design with AI, 3D reconstruction with UAV, or rapid prototyping with 3D printing.

I’m looking for Master and MPhil students with passion in graphics, good coding skill and strong curiosity. Reach me if you are interested.
